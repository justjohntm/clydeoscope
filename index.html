i.

Two practices. One studio.

Studio

We design and build. Immersive experiences, wellness environments, creative technology and digital products all rooted in how the nervous system actually responds to its environment.

Neuroaesthetics is the science of how the brain and body respond to art, culture, design and environment. We apply that science to everything we make so the work doesn't just look right, it feels right.

Lab

Every experience we build is also a research instrument. Using EEG, biofeedback and physiological data, we measure what the work is actually doing to the body and use that to make it better.

We take the neuroscience lab into the real world. Brain-body responses captured in real environments give us an accurate picture of how people feel in the moment not what they say they feel.

Wellness · MVP

The wellness pod

A space to stop, soften, and listen. Sound, light and gentle haptic response work together to settle the nervous system returning you to a more regulated way of being, and a different relationship with your own body and mind. What you leave with is a snapshot of your nervous system. Something worth sitting with.
